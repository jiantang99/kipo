Method 1 
Cut the outer sheath, but do not remove it. Feed the cable 
through the screw connection and then remove the outer sheath. 
Now pull the cable back until the connection is established 
between the cable shield and the contact spring. 
 
Method 2

sheath. Insert the cables into the screw connection until the 
connection is established between the cable shield and the 
contact spring.
