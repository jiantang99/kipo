97 E Pre-Earth FMLB
Amphenol® Pre-Earth/First Mate Last Break Connectors are designed for applications where a protective circuit from the ground 
contact to the shell is critical. These connectors provide a path for any stray voltage to be shunted to a safe ground avoiding 
harm to the operator and the voltage sensitive equipment.
